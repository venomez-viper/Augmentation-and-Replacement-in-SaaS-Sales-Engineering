# Systematic Review of Particle Swarm Optimization: Methods and Applications

## Cite_Key
gad2022particle

## Problem
PSO has undergone extensive modification since the 1990s, but a systematic up-to-date review of its variants, hybridizations, and real-world applications was lacking.

## Method
Systematic Review (SR) methodology applied to PSO literature from 2017–2019; taxonomic analysis of hybridization, improvement, variants, and applications in healthcare, environmental, industrial, commercial, smart city, and general domains.

## Data
Published PSO research (2017–2019) identified via systematic search.

## Metrics
Accuracy, evaluation environments, and proposed case study characteristics across reviewed studies.

## Findings
PSO has been successfully applied across diverse domains; hybridization and parameter tuning remain active research areas; open issues include convergence guarantees and high-dimensional scalability.

## Limitations
Coverage restricted to 2017–2019 literature; rapid algorithm development means some advances may be missing.

## Citation
Gad (2022). Particle Swarm Optimization Algorithm and Its Applications: A Systematic Review. Archives of Computational Methods in Engineering. https://doi.org/10.1007/s11831-021-09694-4

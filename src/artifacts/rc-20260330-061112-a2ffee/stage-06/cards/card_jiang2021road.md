# 6G: Drivers, Use Cases, Requirements, Architecture, and Enabling Technologies

## Cite_Key
jiang2021road

## Problem
The field lacked a comprehensive, quantitative survey defining 6G relative to 5G and mapping the full landscape of candidate technologies and standardization roadmaps.

## Method
Comprehensive survey covering 6G driving factors (traffic growth to 2030), use cases, KPIs (quantitatively compared with 5G), research activities by institution/country, standardization roadmap, and ~12 candidate technologies.

## Data
Literature synthesis from academia and industry up to 2021.

## Metrics
Quantitative KPI comparisons (latency, throughput, reliability, energy efficiency) between 5G and projected 6G targets.

## Findings
6G will likely achieve Tbps peak rates, sub-ms latency, and near-100% coverage; AI-native networks, THz bands, and intelligent reflecting surfaces are leading candidates; standardization expected around 2030.

## Limitations
Roadmap projections are speculative; technology selection may shift with early standardization decisions.

## Citation
Jiang et al. (2021). The Road Towards 6G: A Comprehensive Survey. IEEE Open Journal of the Communications Society. https://doi.org/10.1109/ojcoms.2021.3057679

# AI and XAI in Industry 4.0: What, How, and Where

## Cite_Key
ahmed2022artificial

## Problem
Industry 4.0 increasingly relies on AI/ML for autonomous monitoring, predictive maintenance, and decision-making, but lack of explainability limits adoption in high-stakes applications.

## Method
Comprehensive survey of AI and XAI methods in Industry 4.0; covers enabling technologies, ML/DL methods used, XAI approaches (LIME, SHAP, attention, etc.), application domains, and future directions toward human-centric AI.

## Data
Literature review of Industry 4.0 AI/XAI publications up to 2022.

## Metrics
Qualitative taxonomy of methods, application domains, and explainability approaches.

## Findings
AI (especially deep learning) substantially improves predictive maintenance and quality control; XAI methods increase transparency and trust but add computational overhead; human-centric and responsible AI are necessary for high-stakes industrial adoption.

## Limitations
XAI method benchmarking is fragmented; faithfulness of explanations is not always validated; many XAI methods evaluated only in controlled settings.

## Citation
Ahmed et al. (2022). From Artificial Intelligence to Explainable Artificial Intelligence in Industry 4.0. IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics. https://doi.org/10.1109/tii.2022.3146552

# 6G Vision, Requirements, Key Technologies, and Testbeds

## Cite_Key
wang2023road

## Problem
5G faces challenges that necessitate research into 6G; no consensus yet exists on 6G vision, requirements, or enabling technologies.

## Method
Comprehensive survey reviewing published white papers and technical literature; covers 6G vision, architecture, key technologies (AI, THz, RIS, etc.), and existing testbeds; first detailed coverage of 6G verification platforms.

## Data
Literature synthesis from international industrial and academic sources up to 2023.

## Metrics
Technical KPIs compared across generations; qualitative taxonomy of technologies and testbeds.

## Findings
6G will require AI-native design, THz communications, reconfigurable intelligent surfaces, and semantic/goal-oriented communications; many open research issues remain in standardization and verification.

## Limitations
6G standards were still in early definition stage at time of writing; some projected KPIs may shift as ITU-R consensus develops.

## Citation
Wang et al. (2023). On the Road to 6G: Visions, Requirements, Key Technologies, and Testbeds. IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials. https://doi.org/10.1109/comst.2023.3249835

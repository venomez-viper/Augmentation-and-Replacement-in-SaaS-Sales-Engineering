# Model Predictive Control: Engineering Perspective, Theory, and Applications

## Cite_Key
schwenzer2021review

## Problem
Despite a mature theoretical foundation, a comprehensive, application-driven review of MPC covering recent developments and practical implementation strategies was missing.

## Method
Review of MPC theory, historical evolution, and applications across manufacturing engineering domains; includes analysis of computational burden mitigation strategies and practical tuning considerations.

## Data
Literature survey; no new experimental data.

## Metrics
Qualitative taxonomy of MPC variants, application domains, and implementation strategies.

## Findings
MPC can control systems intractable for conventional feedback controllers; physical model insight facilitates tuning; computational burden remains a key design challenge; data-driven and learning-based MPC are emerging trends.

## Limitations
Focus on manufacturing/engineering context may under-represent MPC in other domains (e.g., buildings, chemical processes); computational advances may quickly date implementation recommendations.

## Citation
Schwenzer et al. (2021). Review on model predictive control: an engineering perspective. The International Journal of Advanced Manufacturing Technology. https://doi.org/10.1007/s00170-021-07682-3

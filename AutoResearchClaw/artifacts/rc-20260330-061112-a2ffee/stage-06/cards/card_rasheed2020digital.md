# Digital Twin: Values, Challenges, and Enablers from a Modeling Perspective

## Cite_Key
rasheed2020digital

## Problem
Digital twins promise real-time prediction, optimization, and control of physical assets, but their construction methodology—especially from a modeling perspective—lacks systematic review.

## Method
Review of recent digital twin methodologies: multiphysics solvers, AI integration, big data management, and computational pipelines; covers challenges and enabling technologies with stakeholder recommendations.

## Data
Literature review; no new experimental data.

## Metrics
Qualitative assessment of modeling techniques, data integration approaches, and application domains.

## Findings
Advances in AI and computational pipelines are making digital twins increasingly feasible; key challenges include data quality, model fidelity, real-time synchronization, and cybersecurity; modular multi-disciplinary design is critical.

## Limitations
Rapidly evolving field means specific technology recommendations may age quickly; focus on modeling perspective may underrepresent hardware/deployment challenges.

## Citation
